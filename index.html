About Passport Services

Applying for a passport is a straightforward process that can be completed online. The Passport e-Seva portal provides all the necessary services to apply for a new passport, renew an existing passport, and check the status of your application. We are committed to making the process as smooth and efficient as possible.

To apply for a new passport, click on the "New Passport" link in the navigation bar and fill out the online application form. Ensure you have all the necessary documents ready for upload. For existing passport holders, you can easily renew your passport by clicking on the "Renewal" link and following the instructions provided. If you have any issues or complaints, our customer service is here to help. Use the "Complaint" link to register your issues.

Keep track of your application status and history by clicking on the "History" link. This feature allows you to monitor the progress of your application and ensures you stay informed throughout the process. Our goal is to provide a hassle-free experience for all your passport-related needs.
